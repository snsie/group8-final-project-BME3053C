# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

Our community is dedicated to providing an open and welcoming environment for everyone. All participants are expected to behave respectfully.

Standards
- Be considerate and respectful in communications.
- Refrain from demeaning or harassing behavior.

Enforcement
- Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported to the maintainers.

For the full text and reporting email, adapt the Contributor Covenant template as needed.
